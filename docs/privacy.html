We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. Data is stored and processed using Supabase backend services, and sensitive information is encrypted in transit.

We use trusted third-party services to provide parts of our app, including:

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
